Refund Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2024

At Matrix Solutions, we strive to provide our customers with the best experience possible. We understand that there may be instances where you may need to request a refund. Below is our detailed Refund Policy, which outlines the conditions under which refunds will be issued, and the procedures you should follow if you believe you are eligible for one.

1. General Conditions

Refunds are only considered under specific circumstances. The following are the general conditions under which a refund may be issued:

  • Unauthorized Charges: If unauthorized charges have been made on your credit/debit card or any other payment method, and the order has not been delivered, you may be eligible for a refund.
  • Order Fulfillment Issues: If we are unable to fulfill your order due to a lack of stock or because the item is no longer available, we will process a refund.

2. Refund Eligibility

If your purchase meets any of the general conditions outlined above and is eligible for a refund, please follow the steps below to initiate the process:

  • Contact Customer Support: Send an email to matrixsolutionscs@gmail.com with your order details, including proof of purchase and an explanation of why you believe you are eligible for a refund.
  • Proof of Purchase: We require a receipt or proof of purchase from Matrix Solutions to process any refunds. Ensure that you include all relevant documentation when contacting us.

3. Refund Processing

Once your refund request is received and verified, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will be applied to your original method of payment.

  • Processing Time: Refunds may take some time to be reflected in your account, depending on your payment provider. Please allow up to 7-10 business days for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account.
  • Late or Missing Refunds: If you haven't received a refund after the processing time has passed, please follow these steps:
    • Check your payment accounts again to ensure the refund was not missed.
    • Contact your credit card company or bank, as it may take additional time for the refund to be officially posted.
    • If you have followed these steps and still have not received your refund, contact us at matrixsolutionscs@gmail.com for further assistance.

4. Additional Conditions

  • Modification of Refund Policy: Matrix Solutions reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time without prior notice. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.
  • Exclusions: Any claims or disputes not explicitly covered in this policy will be handled on a case-by-case basis, at the discretion of Matrix Solutions.
